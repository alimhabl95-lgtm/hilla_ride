Welcome to Hello Tuk-Tuk (“Application”, “Service”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use the Hello Tuk-Tuk mobile application and related services.

By using the Application, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

Hello Tuk-Tuk is operated by Ali Mohammed Habl Al-Isawi, Showmali, Hilla, Babil, Iraq. Email: hellotuktuk3@gmail.com.

Throughout this Privacy Policy, “we”, “us”, and “our” refer to the Service Provider.

2. Information We Collect

Customer information may include: full name, phone number, email address (optional), pickup and destination information, location data during ride requests, ride history, in-app chat messages, and device and usage information.

Driver information may include: full name, phone number, profile photo, government-issued identification documents, vehicle information and registration details, location data while providing transportation services, ride history, and device and usage information.

Automatically collected information may include: IP address, mobile device identifiers, device type and operating system, application usage data, crash reports, and diagnostic information.

3. How We Use Information

  • Create and manage user accounts
  • Verify driver identity and eligibility
  • Connect customers with drivers
  • Facilitate transportation requests
  • Improve application performance
  • Provide customer support
  • Detect fraud and misuse
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Maintain platform security

4. Location Information

The Application uses location data to identify pickup and destination locations, match customers with nearby drivers, facilitate transportation services, and improve service quality. Location access is used only when necessary for transportation-related features.

5. Driver Verification

Drivers may be required to submit identification documents, profile photographs, and vehicle information. These documents are reviewed by the Service Provider for verification purposes before driver accounts are activated.

6. Sharing of Information

We do not sell personal information. We may share information between customers and drivers as necessary to complete a trip, with service providers supporting the Application, with legal authorities when required by law, and to protect safety, rights, and security.

7. Third-Party Services

The Application uses third-party services that may process information according to their own policies, including:

  • Google Firebase (authentication, database, storage, notifications)
  • Google Maps / Google Play Services

8. Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as reasonably necessary to provide the Service, fulfill legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. Driver verification documents may be retained for regulatory, security, and fraud-prevention purposes.

9. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures designed to protect personal information. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure.

10. Your Rights

Subject to applicable law, users may request access to personal information, correction of inaccurate information, deletion of personal information, or account closure. Contact: hellotuktuk3@gmail.com.

11. Children’s Privacy

The Application is not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

12. International Transfers

Information may be processed and stored in countries where our service providers operate. Where required by law, appropriate safeguards will be applied.

13. Changes to This Privacy Policy
