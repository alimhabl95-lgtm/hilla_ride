Full description:
Hello Tuk-Tuk is a local ride-hailing app for tuk-tuk trips in Al-Hashimiya District, Babil Governorate, Iraq.

For passengers:
• Choose your sub-district and set pickup and destination on the map
• See the fare before you book — pay in cash only
• Track your driver live on the map
• Chat with your driver during the trip
• Return to your active ride anytime from the app bar

For drivers:
• Go online in your work area and receive nearby ride requests
• Accept or reject trips with one tap
• Navigate to pickup and destination on the map
• See trip earnings and platform commission clearly

Hello Tuk-Tuk is built for our community — simple, fast, and in Arabic and English.
